Jen Howard

Making the world better through UX.

I'm a UX/UI designer that has taken the reigns of a complex, data-mapping application that lacked visual cohesion and interactive consistency and managed to unify our developers' opposing ideas on icons into a single, cohesive brand pattern. On top of that, I paved the way for A/B Testing, managed constant layout design changes, streamlined task flow, and pushed as many quality of life suggestions as possible.

I always push myself to design systems that have the users satisfaction in mind and I never settle for the status quo. I love the challenge of discovering exactly what a user needs and how to create an experience that gets them to their goals as efficiently as possible.
