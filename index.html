CV

USER RESEARCH

I firmly believe that correctly identifying your target audience in the early stages of a project by using User Research is key in building a focused and robust application.

 

COMMUNICATION

I always prefer spending time face-to-face with clients to build a stronger understanding of their vision and to connect with them on a personal level.

 

WIREFRAMING

The bread and butter of the UX Designer's livelihood. With a wide variety of tools, from pen & paper to using the more interactive InVision platform and Adobe XD, I can rapidly prototype the screen flow of any application.

 

DESIGN

Combining the ingredients of User Research, Wifreframing, great Communication & Feedback streamlines the process of creating the visual assets necessary to bring the application to life. It makes for a pretty solid recipe.
